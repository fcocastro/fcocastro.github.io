Francisco Castro

I'm an assistant professor at UCLA Anderson School of Management. My work focuses on the operations and design of markets with emphasis on their spatial and incentive aspects. I'm particularly interested in the design of optimal pricing policies and contracts that can be used in digital marketplaces such as ride-hailing platforms and ad exchanges.
