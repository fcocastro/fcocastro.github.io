Francisco Castro

Operations • Market Design • Economics of AI— UCLA, Anderson School of Management

I'm an assistant professor at the UCLA Anderson School of Management. I study operational and economic questions at the intersection of operations management, market design, and AI. My current work explores electric vehicle fleets, non-profit pricing & defaults, and how AI is reshaping the way we think about business. I am a graduate of University of Chile and received my Ph.D. from Columbia University's Graduate School of Business in 2019. Before joining UCLA, I was a Post-doctoral research scientist at Uber.
